Data Deletion Policy

Last updated:

Your Right to Delete Your Data

At Auriya Studio, we respect your privacy and your right to control your personal data. This page explains how you can request deletion of your account and all associated data.

Important: Deleting your account is permanent and cannot be undone. All your data, including projects, content, and social media connections, will be permanently deleted.

What Data Will Be Deleted

When you delete your account, we will permanently delete:

  • Account Information: Email address, password, profile information, and authentication data
  • Projects: All projects, project names, descriptions, labels, and metadata
  • Content: All uploaded images, videos, captions, hashtags, and associated files
  • Analysis Data: AI-generated insights, feedback, and recommendations
  • Scheduled Posts: All scheduled and published post records
  • Social Media Connections: Stored access tokens and connection data
  • Usage Data: Usage statistics and analytics associated with your account
  • Trends and Ideas: All saved trends and ideas associated with your projects

What Happens to Your Social Media Accounts

  • Deleting your Auriya Studio account does NOT delete your social media accounts (Instagram, Facebook, YouTube, TikTok)
  • We will disconnect all social media accounts from our service
  • Any content already published to social media platforms will remain on those platforms
  • You can manage or delete content on social media platforms directly through those platforms

How to Delete Your Account and Data

You have several options to request account deletion:

Method 1: Delete Through the App (Recommended)

  1. Open the Auriya Studio app on your device
  2. Navigate to Settings (usually accessible from the main menu or profile)
  3. Scroll to the bottom and tap "Sign Out"
  4. In the sign-out confirmation, select "Delete Account" or "Delete Account and Data"
  5. Confirm the deletion by entering your password or confirming your email
  6. Your account and all data will be scheduled for deletion

Method 2: Request Deletion via Email

  1. Send an email to privacy@auriyastudio.com with the subject line: "Account Deletion Request"
  2. Include the following information:
    • Your registered email address
    • Your account username (if applicable)
    • A clear statement requesting account deletion
    • Confirmation that you understand the deletion is permanent
  3. We will verify your identity and process your request within 30 days

Method 3: Contact Support

  1. Contact our support team through the app's support feature
  2. Request account deletion and provide your account email
  3. Our team will guide you through the verification and deletion process

Deletion Timeline

  • Immediate: Your account will be deactivated immediately upon deletion request
  • Within 24 hours: Your account will be marked for deletion and you will no longer be able to access it
  • Within 30 days: All your data will be permanently deleted from our servers and backups
  • Confirmation: You will receive an email confirmation once deletion is complete

Data Retention Exceptions

In certain circumstances, we may be required to retain some data for legal or legitimate business purposes:

  • Legal Requirements: Data required to comply with legal obligations, resolve disputes, or enforce agreements
  • Backup Systems: Data in backup systems may take up to 90 days to be fully purged
  • Aggregated Data: Anonymized, aggregated data that cannot identify you may be retained for analytics
  • Fraud Prevention: Information related to fraud or security incidents may be retained for investigation

Any retained data will be kept only as long as necessary and will not be used for any other purpose.

Local Data on Your Device

Note: Auriya Studio stores data locally on your device. To completely remove all data:

  1. Delete your account through one of the methods above (this removes cloud data)
  2. Uninstall the Auriya Studio app from your device
  3. Clear app data from your device settings (if available)

Local data stored on your device will be removed when you uninstall the app, but account deletion ensures all cloud-stored data is also removed.

What Happens After Deletion

  • You will no longer be able to sign in to your account
  • All your projects, content, and data will be permanently deleted
  • You can create a new account at any time, but your previous data cannot be recovered
  • Any scheduled posts that haven't been published will be cancelled
  • Social media accounts will be disconnected from our service

Request Status

After submitting a deletion request, you can:

  • Check the status of your request by contacting us at privacy@auriyastudio.com
  • You will receive email updates on the progress of your deletion request
  • If you change your mind, you have 7 days to cancel the deletion request by contacting us

Your Privacy Rights

Depending on your location, you may have additional privacy rights under laws such as:

  • GDPR (General Data Protection Regulation) - European Union
  • CCPA (California Consumer Privacy Act) - California, USA
  • Other applicable data protection laws

We are committed to honoring these rights. If you believe we have not adequately addressed your privacy concerns, you may have the right to file a complaint with your local data protection authority.
