Privacy Policy

Last updated:

1. Introduction

Welcome to Auriya Studio ("we," "our," or "us"). We are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring you have a positive experience while using our social media content management application. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application.

2. Information We Collect

2.1 Information You Provide

  • Account Information: Email address, password (encrypted), and profile information when you create an account
  • Content: Images, videos, captions, hashtags, and other content you upload or create within the app
  • Project Data: Project names, descriptions, labels, and associated content
  • Social Media Connections: Access tokens for connected social media accounts (Instagram, Facebook, YouTube, TikTok) stored securely on your device

2.2 Automatically Collected Information

  • Usage Data: Information about how you interact with the app, including features used, time spent, and actions taken
  • Device Information: Device type, operating system version, and app version
  • Analytics: Aggregated usage statistics to improve our services

2.3 Third-Party Services

  • Supabase: We use Supabase for authentication and data storage. Your account data is stored securely in Supabase's infrastructure.
  • Social Media Platforms: When you connect social media accounts, we access your account information and posting capabilities through OAuth. We do not store your social media passwords.
  • AI Services: We use AI services (Google Gemini, OpenAI, or Perplexity) to analyze your content and generate insights. Media files may be processed by these services.

3. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Provide, maintain, and improve our services
  • Authenticate your identity and manage your account
  • Process and analyze your content using AI services
  • Connect to and post content on your connected social media accounts
  • Schedule and publish posts according to your preferences
  • Send you service-related communications
  • Monitor and analyze usage patterns to improve user experience
  • Detect, prevent, and address technical issues

4. Data Storage and Security

  • Local Storage: Your content, projects, and social media access tokens are stored locally on your device using secure storage mechanisms
  • Cloud Storage: Account information and metadata are stored securely in Supabase's cloud infrastructure
  • Encryption: We use industry-standard encryption to protect your data in transit and at rest
  • Access Tokens: Social media access tokens are stored securely on your device and are user-specific

5. Social Media Integration

When you connect social media accounts:

  • We use OAuth to securely authenticate with social media platforms
  • We request permissions necessary to post content on your behalf
  • Access tokens are stored locally on your device and are not shared with third parties
  • You can disconnect social media accounts at any time through the app settings
  • We only post content when you explicitly schedule or publish it

6. AI Content Analysis

When you use AI-powered features:

  • Your content (images/videos) may be sent to AI service providers for analysis
  • AI services process your content to generate insights, captions, and recommendations
  • We do not use your content to train AI models without your explicit consent
  • AI service providers are bound by their own privacy policies

7. Data Sharing and Disclosure

We do not sell your personal information. We may share your information only in the following circumstances:

  • Service Providers: With third-party service providers who perform services on our behalf (Supabase, AI services)
  • Legal Requirements: When required by law or to protect our rights and safety
  • Business Transfers: In connection with a merger, acquisition, or sale of assets
  • With Your Consent: When you explicitly authorize us to share your information

8. Your Rights and Choices

You have the right to:

  • Access: Request access to your personal data
  • Correction: Update or correct your account information
  • Deletion: Request deletion of your account and associated data (see Data Deletion Policy)
  • Disconnect: Disconnect social media accounts at any time
  • Opt-Out: Disable AI features or limit data collection where possible

9. Data Retention

  • We retain your account information for as long as your account is active
  • Content and projects are stored locally on your device
  • When you delete your account, we delete your data from our servers within 30 days
  • Some data may be retained for legal or legitimate business purposes

10. Children's Privacy

Auriya Studio is not intended for users under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe we have collected information from a child under 13, please contact us immediately.

11. International Data Transfers

Your information may be transferred to and processed in countries other than your country of residence. These countries may have data protection laws that differ from those in your country. By using our services, you consent to the transfer of your information to these countries.

12. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the "Last updated" date. You are advised to review this Privacy Policy periodically for any changes.
